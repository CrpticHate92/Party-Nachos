Party Nachos

Let's learn how to make Party Nachos

Ingredients

  • Tortilla Chips
  • Cheese
  • Bell Peppers
  • Jalapeños
  • Ground Beef
  • Olives
  • Salsa
  • Sour Cream

Instructions

  1. Cook the ground beef so that it isn't raw.
  2. Add some chips to a plate.
  3. Sprinkle some cheese on them.
  4. Microwave it for about 30sec-1min
  5. Cut up your bell peppers and add them on the plate.
  6. Add the ground beef you cooked onto the plate.
  7. Put some of the olives on to the plate.
  8. Spread some salsa and sour cream over the nachos.
  9. Cut up your Jalapeños and add them on the very top.
  10. Sprinkle some more cheese on the plate.
  11. Enjoy!
